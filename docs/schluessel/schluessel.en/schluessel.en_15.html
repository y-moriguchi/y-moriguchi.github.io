3.9 Mails

Function: send-mail hostname [port] (to …) (cc …) (bcc …) subject text

sends a mail to the hostname.

Function: set-send-mail-encoding! encoding

sets the default encoding for sending a mail.

Function: send-mail-encoding

gets the default encoding for sending a mail.
