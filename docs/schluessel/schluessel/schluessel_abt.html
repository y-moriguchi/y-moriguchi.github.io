ボタン 名称 移動先 1.2.3項からの移動先

[上] Up Up section 1.2
[ > ] Forward Next section in reading order 1.2.4
[ >> ] FastForward Next chapter 2
[冒頭] 冒頭 Cover (top) of document  
[目次] 目次 Table of contents  
[見出し] 見出し 見出し  
[ ? ] About About (help)
